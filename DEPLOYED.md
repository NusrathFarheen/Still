## How It Detects Objects (Mock AI):

The app analyzes your image:
- **Bright + Warm** = Sky or sunset
- **Warm + Soft** = Person or coffee
- **High detail + Texture** = Tree or building
- **Cool + Dark** = Night scene
- **Everything else** = Book, flower, street, or window

It's not perfect but it's smart enough to feel magical! When you add real AI later, it'll be even better.
